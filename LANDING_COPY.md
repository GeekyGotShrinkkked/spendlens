## FAQ

### Q1: Is this actually free?
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no account,
no hidden fees. SpendLens is a free tool built by
Credex to help startups spend smarter on AI.

### Q2: How accurate is the audit?
Every recommendation is based on official vendor
pricing pages verified during submission week.
The audit engine uses financially defensible logic —
not guesswork. A finance person should read our
reasoning and agree with it.

### Q3: Do you store my data?
We store your audit results so you can share them
via a unique URL. If you enter your email, we store
that too. We never sell your data. Ever.

### Q4: What tools does SpendLens cover?
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API,
OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools coming soon.

### Q5: What is Credex?
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits —
Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise and others —
sourced from companies that overforecast or pivoted.
The discount is real and substantial. SpendLens helps
you find where you're overspending. Credex helps you
capture those savings.
